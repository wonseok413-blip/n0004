Website Security Reality

The Threats Are Real. The Damage Is Fast.

Website powers 43% of the web making it the #1 target for malware, brute force attacks, and exploitation. Understanding the threat landscape is the first step to protecting your business.

Backdoor Shells

Hidden PHP files that give attackers persistent access to your server surviving even after a standard malware removal. Requires manual expert detection and elimination.

SEO Spam Injections

Thousands of spam pages injected into your site, destroying search rankings overnight. Google blacklists follow often causing 90%+ organic traffic loss within days.

Credit Card Skimmers

JavaScript injected into WooCommerce checkout pages silently captures payment data. Legal liability, GDPR breaches, and PCI compliance failures result from brief exposure.

Malicious Redirects

Your site silently sends visitors to scam or pharmaceutical sites. Search engines penalize immediately recovery without expert intervention takes weeks.
