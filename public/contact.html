We require FTP, SFTP, or SSH access to your hosting server, along with your Website admin credentials. Once you submit a cleanup request, our team will securely connect to your server and begin the remediation process. All credentials are handled with strict confidentiality.

Most malware cleanups are completed within 24 hours. Emergency requests are prioritized and typically resolved within 6-12 hours. The exact timeline depends on the severity of the infection and the complexity of your site's architecture.

Our team takes every precaution to preserve your site's functionality. We back up all modified files before making changes. Our firewall solution requires only a simple DNS change no software installation needed on your server.
