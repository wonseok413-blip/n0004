Before You Contact

Quick Answers

How quickly will I hear back?

General inquiries receive a response within 24 hours (usually much faster). Emergency malware requests are prioritized and typically get an initial response within 1-3 hours.

What information should I include in my message?

For malware emergencies: your website URL, symptoms, and any error messages. For other services: your website URL (if applicable), project scope, timeline, and budget range.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! Initial consultations are always free. We'll discuss your needs, assess your situation, and provide honest recommendations—no obligation.

What if my issue is urgent but outside business hours?

Mark your inquiry as "Emergency" in the form or include "EMERGENCY" in your email subject line. Our on-call team monitors for urgent requests 24/7 and will respond within 3-4 hours maximum.
