When Do You Need a Rebuild?

Outdated Technology

Using deprecated PHP versions, old WordPress core, or unsupported themes/plugins that create severe security vulnerabilities and compatibility issues.

Repeated Infections

Site keeps getting reinfected despite cleanup attempts. The codebase may be too compromised to safely maintain without complete reconstruction.

Performance Issues

Slow loading times, poor mobile performance, or accumulated technical debt that can't be resolved through optimization alone. Rebuild offers clean start.

Design Refresh Needed

Outdated design that doesn't reflect your current brand, poor user experience, low conversion rates, or non-responsive mobile layout.

Maintenance Nightmare

Overly complex custom code, plugin conflicts, or poorly documented modifications making updates risky and time-consuming. Fresh start saves long-term costs.

Business Evolution

Your business has grown beyond original site capabilities. Need e-commerce, memberships, or advanced features that current platform can't support effectively.
