Common Questions

Package FAQs

Are these one-time or recurring payments?

One-time. You pay once for the setup, and the improvements stay with your site permanently. We offer optional ongoing monitoring separately if you want continued protection.

How long does the setup take?

Performance Boost and Security Hardening are typically completed within 1–3 business days. The combined Performance + Security package takes 2–4 business days depending on site complexity.

Will my site be taken offline during setup?

No. All configuration work is done on your live site with minimal disruption. For complex changes, we may schedule a brief maintenance window (under 30 minutes) in off-peak hours.

What if I need malware removal first?

We recommend cleaning the site before optimizing it. Contact us — we can bundle malware removal with a performance/security package at a combined rate.

Do you offer a guarantee?

Yes. We provide a before/after speed report to document improvements. If the configured settings cause any issues, we resolve them at no additional charge within 30 days of delivery.
