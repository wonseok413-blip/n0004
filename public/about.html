Our Journey

From Emergency Response to Complete Solutions

The Beginning: Malware Crisis Response

Noteracker was born from a simple observation: businesses facing malware infections needed immediate, professional helpnot tomorrow, not next week, but right now. We saw too many small businesses and entrepreneurs lose revenue, reputation, and rankings while waiting for traditional IT support.

Building on Sucuri's Foundation

We partnered with Sucuri, the industry's most respected malware detection and removal platform, to provide enterprise-grade security services to businesses of all sizes. This partnership enables us to detect threats with unprecedented accuracy and remove them with surgical precision.

Expanding Our Mission

As we helped more clients recover from infections, we realized many faced recurring issues: outdated plugins, poor hosting, lack of maintenance. This insight led us to develop comprehensive Website management services that prevent problems before they start.

Today: Full-Spectrum Website Expertise

While malware care remains our foundation and specialty, we now offer complete Website solutions: from design and development to hosting and ongoing maintenance. Every service we provide carries the same commitment to security, performance, and reliability that built our reputation in emergency response.
